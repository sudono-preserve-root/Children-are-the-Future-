Our Services

CATF delivers comprehensive programs focused on education, well-being, and growth for children in underserved communities.

Education Community Food Support Teacher Training Family Support

Education Access

We provide essential tools and materials to ensure equitable access to quality education.

  • Book drives (local and international)
  • Notebook, pencil, and backpack distribution
  • School partnerships and scholarships

Community Engagement

We mobilize community support through local outreach and collaborative events.

  • Volunteer and donor programs
  • Local partner campaigns
  • Annual book and food drives

Food Support

We ensure children and families have access to nutritious meals inside and outside of school.

  • Emergency food relief efforts
  • School lunch program collaboration
  • Seasonal food and water drives

Continuing Education for Teachers

We support educators through professional development and digital integration workshops.

  • Workshops in modern teaching methods
  • Digital learning tools and training
  • Peer-led curriculum planning

Support for Families

Our wraparound services provide stability and care beyond the classroom.

  • Parent and caregiver workshops
  • Clothing and hygiene drives
  • Safe learning environment initiatives
