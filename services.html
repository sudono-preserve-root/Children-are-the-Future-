Our Services

CATF delivers comprehensive support systems focused on the education, well-being, and growth of children in underserved communities.

Education Access

We provide tools and support to underfunded and rural schools.

  • Book drives (local & international)
  • Notebooks, pencils, backpacks
  • School partnerships

Community Engagement

Mobilizing local volunteer and donor-led campaigns.

  • Book and food drives
  • Volunteer programs
  • Partner-led campaigns

Food Support

We ensure families and students are well nourished year-round.

  • Emergency food relief
  • School lunch programs
  • Community food donations

Continuing Education for Teachers

We equip teachers with tools for inclusive and tech-enhanced classrooms.

  • Technology integration workshops
  • Curriculum development
  • Student-centered learning

Support for Communities

We uplift whole communities through safety, health, and family programs.

  • Hygiene and clothing drives
  • Parent & caregiver workshops
  • Safe learning environments
